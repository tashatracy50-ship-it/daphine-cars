Get to know Kampala Auto Spares

Who We Are

Kampala Auto Spares is a locally owned and operated business specializing in high-quality car spare parts for a wide range of vehicle models. Located in the heart of Kampala, Uganda, we have proudly served our community since 2015, becoming a trusted name in automotive solutions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide reliable, affordable, and genuine spare parts that keep your vehicle running smoothly. We believe in building long-term relationships with our customers through honesty, professionalism, and exceptional service.
